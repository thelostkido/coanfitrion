Rentberry Announces $12.4M RegA+ Capital Raise

Ad

To all our inventors, partners, and users, we are happy to announce that Rentberry has launched a new investment campaign. We have an ambitious goal to raise $12.4M that will fuel our growth and allow us to implement two new projects and introduce some brand-new solutions to Rentberry.

Thanks to earlier fundraising rounds and the support of our celebrated investors, we were able to achieve tremendous results. We’ve expanded our presence to 30+ countries, ensured partnerships with a few key players in the real estate industry, and created some of Rentberry’s most notable features, such as virtual tours and all-encompassing tenant screening. 

We want to assure you that our team has an incredible vision of what’s ahead — from the new project Flexible Living to proving Rentberry as one of the leading companies in the rental market worldwide!

Flexible Living in a Nutshell

First, let us introduce you to Flexible Living  — a new approach to mid-term renting. This project was inspired by the shift to remote work and the ability to live and work from anywhere in the world. With Flexible Living, renters can enjoy flexibility by renting properties for three to twelve months in the top locations around the globe. What’s more, these properties will be fully furnished and require no security deposit — a perfect solution for any digital nomad.

And What About Rentberry?

The steady development of the Rentberry platform is our utmost priority. We believe that the new investments will fuel our growth and help us achieve quicker some of the team’s most exciting ideas and aspirations.

Here are just a few elements for which we’re planning to dedicate funding:

  • shifting to the freemium model
  • increasing user base
  • adding new features
  • acquiring listings in new countries and locations

As you can see, we have remarkable plans, and we are thrilled to see what the future holds for Rentberry!
