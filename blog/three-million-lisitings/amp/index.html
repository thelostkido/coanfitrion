Security improvement through Stripe

Additionally, our development team works hard on protecting the Rentberry platform from scams and enhancing payment security. Starting now, we’ll be processing all payments through Stripe — an incredibly secure software used by millions of companies around the globe. This will allow us to reduce the number of fraudulent transactions by introducing a more comprehensive check of any payer’s identity.

Virtual tours update

And, finally, we keep introducing minor improvements when it comes to the Virtual Tours feature. From now on, renters can use a new search filter to look through only those properties offering virtual tours. We believe this will make a search for a new home more enjoyable and convenient!
