Still Have Any Questions?

Is Rentberry.com safe for tenant screening?

Rentberry's tenant screening is powered by the top credit reporting agency Experian. Our platform does not store, share, or sell any sensitive information provided during the screening.

 What does Rentberrys tenant screening include?

Our tenant screening consists of two comprehensive reports credit report and background check. We provide the information including, but not limited to, rental history, employment information, credit score and factors influencing it, bankruptcies, collections, and public records.

 How much does it cost?

Rentberry tenant screening services are free for landlords. Tenants would pay $19.99 for both reports, and it can be easily done with a credit card.

 What is a credit report and background check?

The credit report shows details of the potential tenants payment history, while the background check reveals employment history, eviction history, and criminal record.

 How to check your credit report?

When it comes to the credit reports, we present information for the past 24 months.
